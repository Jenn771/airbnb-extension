Setup Instructions
Perfect for flexible travelers: Don't know exact dates but know how many nights you want? This finds the cheapest dates for you!
1
Setup Airbnb first:
In Airbnb's date picker, click "Flexible"
Select "Weekend" or "Week" (not "month")
Optionally, choose which months you're open to
Click "Search" to view listings
2
Use this extension:
Enter how many nights you want to stay
Click "Find Best Dates" to see cheapest options
Important: This extension only works with Airbnb's "Flexible" date searches, not exact dates
