Best Dates Finder
Find cheapest flexible dates
Setup Instructions
Perfect for flexible travelers: Don't know eact dates but know how many nights you want? This finds the cheapest dates for you!
Setup Airbnb first:
1. In Airbnb's date picker, click "Flexible"
2. Select "Weekend" or "Week" (not "month")
3. Optionally, choose which months you're open to
4. Click "Search" to view listings
Use this extension:
5. Enter how many nights you want to stay
6. Click "Find Best Dates" to see cheapest options
Important: This extension only works with Airbnb's "Flexible" date searches, not exact dates
